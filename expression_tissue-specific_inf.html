The heatmaps in this section is made for the lncRNAs which express highly in a certain tissue. We also offer files with the average expression level of tissue-specific lncRNAs. Since there are quite a large number of tissue-specific lncRNAs in testis and liver, the heatmaps of testis and liver in display are vague and we offer clear heatmaps for downloading. There are ~10000 testis- specific lncRNAs and the ones with the 500 highest expression level are in the heatmap of testis.
